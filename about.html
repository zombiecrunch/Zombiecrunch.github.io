About Me

I've made a career out of trial by fire, or perhaps I should say, many trials and many fires taught me some things. Like how to listen. And how to treat people. I believe in building teams through encouragement and consistent coaching. I believe great work happens when people are free to create, which means creating smarter processes that enable them.

I've done standup comedy, and I write music, books, TV scripts and anything else that attracts my ADHD. I love to laugh. I embrace new technologies. I love new challenges.

If that sounds like something you're looking for, my door is always open.

(I don't really drink though, so you'll have to be content if I just slowly sip that beer.)
