Positioning and Brand Development

Business is about getting people to buy. Whether building a business from the ground up, or rethinking who you are, or even uncovering a why that's been buried under years of business-as-usual, you've got to be able to articulate who you are and who you're not.

I've led many brands through positioning workshops, helping them shake off the dust with new and powerful positions that reinvigorated their organizations and made better, smarter propositions to their customers.
